# Insta Buddy Mart 🛒

**Insta Buddy Mart** is a community-driven delivery platform designed to bridge the gap between people who can't leave their homes and neighbors who are already out shopping. 

Unlike traditional delivery services, Insta Buddy Mart leverages "existing trips," making delivery more affordable for the requester and more efficient for the environment.

## 🌟 Key Features
* **Post Requests:** Users can create a shopping list for specific marts/stores when they are unable to go themselves.
* **Neighbor Discovery:** Users already at or heading to a store can see nearby requests on a live map or list.
* **In-App Checklist:** Helpers use a real-time checklist to ensure accuracy while shopping.
* **Secure Payments:** Integrated payment system that handles both the cost of goods and the "helper's fee."
* **In-App Chat:** Easy communication for item substitutions or delivery instructions.

## 🤝 How It Works
1.  **The Requester:** Posts a list of items needed and offers a small delivery incentive.
2.  **The Runner:** Browses local requests. If they are already going to that store, they "Accept" the task.
3.  **The Shop:** The Helper buys the items along with their own groceries.
4.  **The Hand-off:** The Helper drops off the items, and the payment is released.
